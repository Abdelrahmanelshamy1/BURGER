How to make a burger sandwich

The burger sandwich is one of the most famous and beloved sandwich worldwide. It is almost impossible to find someone who don't like burger. So, why don't we try make it our own at home with simple ingredients, easiness and deliciousness. Let's see how to make this exciting sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bun
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • onion
  • sauce
  • cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Chop the onions, tomatos, and the lettuce
  2. Cook the burger
  3. Put the cheddar cheese on the burger to melt
  4. Toast the bun
  5. Put the sauce on the bun and put the components in layers as you wish
